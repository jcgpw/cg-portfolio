About

Greetings! I'm Jed, a Technical Artist with a vested interest in Game Development and all things CG.

I've always had an interest in video games and mostly have my roots on gaming on the PC as early as I could remember (about 6 or so years of age). Some of the earlier video games I played were Sim City 3000, Daggerfall, and Doom; therefore a lot of the genres I've also grown to enjoy has stemmed from these games.

Eventually, I grew fond of modding video games–starting with the first Sims game. This love and deep interest for this pastime are what pushed me into the field of computer graphics and also is what is driving my passions to go into being an indie game developer.

I’m currently a freelancer and student engaged in creating 3d video game art assets together with an effective skillset in computer graphics programming as well.
